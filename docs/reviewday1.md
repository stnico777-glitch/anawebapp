Awake & Align: Development Plan - Post Review Call

This document outlines the refined development plan and immediate next steps for the Awake & Align project, incorporating feedback and decisions from the review call held on February 9, 2026. The goal remains a fully functional iOS application and web platform by April 11, 2026, with the Android application following by Week 14.

1. Summary of Review Call Outcomes

The first review call was highly productive, with positive reception of the initial foundational build for both the web and app platforms. Key creative and functional directions were confirmed, and specific refinements were identified to ensure alignment with the Awake & Align vision. The team expressed enthusiasm for the project's progress.

2. Key Design & Feature Refinements

Based on the feedback received, the following design and feature adjustments will be implemented in the next iteration:

2.1. Homepage / App Entry Experience

•
Consolidated Call-to-Action: The initial two buttons will be streamlined into a single, compelling call-to-action, such as "Begin Your Journey," to guide users more effectively. This aims to simplify the onboarding process and reduce decision fatigue for new users.

•
Personalized Welcome: A personalized welcome message from Kat, such as "Hey girl, so glad you're here," will be integrated to foster a warmer, more engaging initial user experience. This aligns with the brand's mission of personal connection.

•
Scrolling Banner: The existing scrolling banner, featuring a Bible verse and promotional content, was approved and will be maintained. This element provides dynamic content delivery and reinforces the spiritual core of the platform.

2.2. Weekly Schedule Module

•
Enhanced Layout: The weekly schedule will be redesigned to explicitly display Monday through Saturday, with a distinct card or entry dedicated to "Sabbath" for Sunday. This provides a clear, comprehensive overview of the weekly routine.

•
Content Access Control: A prominent "lock" icon will be implemented on all content inaccessible to non-subscribed users. This clearly communicates premium content and encourages subscription conversions.

2.3. Feature Navigation Section

•
Mobile Optimization: For mobile interfaces, the current vertical list of features will be re-engineered into a horizontal, scrollable section. Individual feature cards will also be reduced in size to optimize screen real estate and improve navigation on smaller devices.

•
Primary Navigation Structure: The main navigation will be standardized across platforms to include: Weekly Schedule, Workout Library, Audio Library, Community, and a More button. This ensures consistency and ease of access to core functionalities.

2.4. Audio Library Enhancements

•
Categorization: The Audio Library will be structured with filterable sub-categories, including Prayer, Affirmations, and Breathing. This allows users to easily find specific content and accommodates the upcoming Christian breathwork content.

2.5. "More" Tab for Spiritual Growth

•
New Section: A new section or tab labeled "More" will be introduced. This area will serve as a dedicated space for users seeking deeper spiritual guidance, including resources for salvation prayers and next steps in their faith journey.

2.6. Journal Feature Integration

•
App-Centric Design: The Journal will remain a core, interactive feature exclusively within the logged-in app experience. On the public-facing website, the Journal will be mentioned as a feature but will not be an interactive component, directing users to the app for this functionality.
